Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to RSVP to attend the day of?

Yep, you sure do. Make sure to RSVP to get a special link that will allow you to create your profile on Hopin and also receive reminder emails.

What platform is Techqueria using to host the summit?

We are using Hopin.to which supports a main stage, expo booths, 1:1 networking sessions and panel sessions.

Can I just attend one day or one session?

Yes, the event is free to attend so please attend what sessions are most appealing to you! You will still need to RSVP if you want to attend any session though.

Is there a Code of Conduct?

Yes, please refer to Techqueria's Code of Conduct. You will be booted out of the event if you violate it.

How do I convince my manager to let me attend?

Check out this sample copy we've created that you can share with your manager.

Will there be swag?

But of course! Not everyone will get swag but we'll be definitely be giving a couple of random lucky winners some awesome items that get shipped directly to them.

Can I sponsor this event?

Yes, but we have limited sponsorship options starting September 12th and will stop taking requests on September 30th. Reach out to us at partnerships@techqueria.org if interested.

Can I be a speaker?

We closed our call for speakers starting September 12th so unfortunately, no but please feel free to submit your info for consideration for other Techqueria events.

Do speakers receive an honorarium?

Yes, of course. We value the time and effort it takes to create engaging content for our attendees to interact with. The honorarium is $100 for panelists and $250 for those who are the sole speaker for their session.

How many attendees will there be?

We are expecting over 1,000 RSVPs and at least 300 folks in attendance at any given time.

How can I share this event?

Check out the #LHM Summit Social Media Kit for images and copy you can use to share the event with friends.

I still have questions!

Reach out to us at support@techqueria.org or chat with us directly through our website for general questions.
