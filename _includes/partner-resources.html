Aspire to Tech Scholarship Program

Udacity and AT&T, are offering over 500 scholarships aimed at increasing diversity and offering economic opportunities to students from disadvantaged socioeconomic groups. Scholarship recipients can choose to learn in one of these three cutting edge programs: Intro to Programming, Front End Web Development, and Android Basics

Interested students can learn more about the program and apply directly through the scholarship webpage here. There's a bit of a tight timeline as applications are open only until Tuesday, June 6. so apply quickly!. Recipients will be notified on June 16 and class will start on June 19.

In the application, it’s crucial for students to list out the name of their affiliated organization. There is an option for “other;” applicants should choose this and then put 'techqueria' in the following question. Likewise, for the 3 short essays (150 words each), they encourage applicants to really make the case for who they are, why they need this scholarship, how they’d use the skills they learn to build a career in tech. This is a great opportunity for applicants to show us they’ll be an engaged and motivated student.

Please direct any questions directly to scholarships@udacity.com
